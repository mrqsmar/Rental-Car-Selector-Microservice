You have chosen a Compact Luxury Car. Cars in this segment
are entry level luxury and are decently equipped. Cars in this 
segment include the Alfa Romeo Giulia, Audi A3, A4, BMW 330i, Cadillac CT4, Genesis G70,
Lexus IS350 F-Sport, Infiniti Q50S, MB A220, C300 and Volvo S60. 
All of these cars except the A220 and Q50S are great choices. But my pick would be
the Giulia or the A4. The Giulia is a very rare car, and handles corners 
and accelerates very well. The A4 is much more luxurious and has a beautiful interior.
